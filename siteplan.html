Discover the Democratic Republic of the Congo

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Discover the Democratic Republic of the Congo

This name was chosen because the website is designed to help visitors learn about the Democratic Republic of the Congo, including its provinces, culture, languages, cities, wildlife, and tourist attractions.

Optional Domain: discoverdrc.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce the Democratic Republic of the Congo and provide information about its provinces, major cities, culture, languages, tourist attractions, wildlife, and photo gallery. The site will also include interactive features developed with JavaScript to improve the visitor experience.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular tourist attractions in the Democratic Republic of the Congo?
  • What languages are spoken in the DRC and where are they commonly used?
  • What are the major cities and provinces of the country?
  • What wildlife species can visitors see in Congolese national parks?

Color Schema

Dark Green (#006633) - Used for headings, navigation, buttons, and footer.


Gold (#FFD700) - Used for highlights, accents, and hover effects.


Light Gray (#F4F4F4) - Used for page backgrounds and content sections.

Typography

Poppins - Used for headings and section titles.

Roboto - Used for body text and general content.
